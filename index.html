Chaowei Xiao

Email: xiaocw [at] umich [dot] edu

I am Chaowei Xiao, a research scientist at NVIDIA Research. I received my PhD degree from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor under supervision of Professor Mingyan Liu . I obtained my bachelor's degree in School of Software from Tsinghua University in 2015. I was also a visiting student at UC Berkeley in 2018, advised by Professor Dawn Song and Professor Bo Li.

My research interest includes Trustworhty Machine Learning, Security & Privacy, IoT/CPS.
