Chaowei Xiao

Email: xiaocw [at] umich [dot] edu

I am Chaowei Xiao, an assistant professor in the School of Computing, Informatics, and Decision Systems Engineering at Arizona State University and also a research scientist at NVIDIA Research.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer security, privacy, and machine learning, with the goal to build the soically responsible machine learning systems.


I obtained my PhD degree from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor under supervision of Peter and Evelyn Fuss Chair of Electrical and Computer Engineering Prof. Mingyan Liu . I obtained my bachelor's degree in School of Software from Tsinghua University in 2015, advised by Professor Yunhao Liu. I was also a visiting student at UC Berkeley in 2018, advised by Professor Dawn Song.

[Google Scholar]


My group has multiple positions for PhD student, Summer interns and Visiting students. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV, and publications (if any).
